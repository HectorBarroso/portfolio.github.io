Hamburger

  • Quantity: 8 hamburgers
  • Time: 15-30 minuts

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount Price
Minced meat 1kg 8,10€/kg
Onion 1 2,29€/kg
Egg 1 0,3€/kg
Cloves of garlic 3 3,99€/kg
Fresh parsley 3 branches 51,60€/Kg
Salt 1 g 1,55€/Kg
Black pepper 1 g 18,50€/Kg
Mustard sauce 1 tablespoon 8,60€/Kg
Bread 1 2,50€/Kg
Olive oil 1 tablespoon 8,94€/l

Steps to follow

  1. We begin to chop the ingredients: first the onion very finely and then the garlic cloves, also in small pieces.
  2. We continue finely chopping the fresh parsley. Once all these ingredients have been chopped, we reserve them.
  3. Season the meat to taste. We place this in a container large enough to allow us to work comfortably with it later. We drain the bread and place it next to the meat. We also add the egg, onion and garlic, parsley and mustard sauce
  4. All the ingredients must be mixed well so that there is a homogeneous mass.
  5. Once you have obtained this dough, you have to make medium balls with the meat, as if they were meatballs, but instead of leaving them completely round, flatten them a little. This way we will achieve the characteristic shape of the hamburgers.
  6. In a large frying pan, heat oil. When it is hot enough, add the burgers and fry them until golden brown to taste.
  7. Once they are fried, remove them from the heat and place them on a plate.
  8. Enjoy

Hot dog

  • Persons: 2
  • Time: 20 minuts

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount Price
German sausages 2 2,63€/kg
Dog buns 2 2,5€/kg
Sweet onion 2 3,78 €/kg
Cornstarch 1 tablespoon 10.72€/Kg
Mayonnaise 1 tablespoon 7,22€/L
Ketchup 1 tablespoon 6,63€/L
Mustard 1 tablespoon 9,96€/L
Pickles 3 6,53€/kg

Steps to follow

  1. We start by making the fried onion. We cut it into brunoise and put it in a tupperware.
  2. We add a tablespoon of cornstarch flour and stir well so that everything is coated evenly.
  3. Fry in plenty of oil until golden brown and remove to absorbent paper. We let it cool and reserve.
  4. We grill the sausage we have chosen. We make some cuts so that it is done better inside.
  5. We toast the bread in that same pan with a small touch of butter.
  6. We serve the dog, the sausage inside and top with the fried onion, the sauces that we like and sliced pickles.
  7. Enjoy

Spaghetti carbonara

  • Persons: 2
  • Time: 20 minuts

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount Price
Spaghetti 170g 2,01€/Kg
Parmesan Cheese 50g 26,28€/Kg
Egg yolk 2 0,3€/Kg
Black pepper To your taste 18,50€/Kg
Bacon 100g 7,69€/kg

Steps to follow

  1. To begin, we put plenty of water with salt in a large pot.
  2. Cut the bacon into strips and brown it in a pan. We reserve the bacon when it is cooked
  3. On the other hand, in a bowl we beat the yolks of the two eggs and add the grated cheese along with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  4. When the water boils, we add the pasta, we eat for the time indicated on the package so that it is al dente and we drain it.
  5. Without letting the pasta cool, we put it back in the casserole and mix it with the beaten egg and cheese.
  6. These ingredients will cook with the remaining heat of the pasta, which will take on its characteristic yellow color. We add the pancetta that you had browned, a little more pecorino or parmesan cheese and finish with a few turns of the pepper mill to give our carbonara the final touch.
  7. Enjoy
